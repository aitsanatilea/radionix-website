Solid state broadband power amplifier

SHUM 2x10

Description

Designed for use as a terminal power amplifier in ultra-wideband communication systems and radar.

SHUM 2х10 includes:

  • smooth phase shifter input signal 0-180o;
  • built-in secondary power supply (possibility to supply low-power external devices)
  • detector of the envelope of the input and output signals with a video signal band of at least 20 MHz on the output of the device;
  • analog attenuator at the input of the device with an adjustment range of at least 35 dB controlled by 0…-3V;
  • built-in protection against overheating with power failure and hysteresis of 2oС;
  • generalized control of readiness with the issuance of an "accident" signal.

About

Radionix LLC is one of the most dynamically developing companies of Ukraine in the market of development, modernization and production of radar, navigation and radio communication systems. The enterprise is focused on development of the newest radar control systems for air traffic and landing, meteorological radars using the modern microelement base and high technologies at all stages of design and serial production of high technology products.

The main company's activities are:

  • Modernization of the radar control systems for air traffic and landing
  • Meteorological radars development and production
  • Development of radar systems for synthesizing aperture

In addition to functionally complete systems, the company introduces to the market serial microwave modules and integrated products for various purposes in the frequency range up to 40 GHz:

  • Precision low-noise coherent master oscillators blocks for pulse-Doppler radar systems
  • Ultra-wideband blocks for the formation of complexes of interference signals with AMI, AM and FM
  • Ultra-wideband low-noise power amplifiers up to 200 W/octave
  • Transceiver devices for communication, radar systems and electronic warfare complexes
  • Digital receivers with an instant bandwidth analysis up to ΔFmg = 1 GHz and the implementation of real-time fast Fourier transform algorithms
  • Blocks for the radar information digital processing with the digital synthesis algorithms implementation for antenna aperture and spectral-correlation analysis

Since its inception, the company has taken a course to create competitive complex products that are not inferior in terms of their technical characteristics to the best world models. The high scientific and technical potential, availability of material, technical and test facilities as well as technological capabilities allow the enterprise to carry out a full cycle of development, testing and production of the most complex products of modern radio electronic equipment for mass production.

The company is always ready for cooperation and partnership in the field of creation and production of modern radio electronic systems for various purposes.
